Ranger Demo — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Ranger Demo is a private, developer-mode Alexa skill built by Bauer Media's Project Ranger team as an internal demonstration. It is not published to the Alexa Skills Store and runs only on the developer's own Amazon account.

What this skill accesses

  • Alexa account and device identifiers, request timestamps, locale, and the intents/slots you speak — provided automatically by Alexa.
  • Your first-line postcode and email address — only if you explicitly consent during the skill.
  • A resettable, non-personal advertising identifier, used to illustrate standard ad frequency-capping.

How it is used

Solely to demonstrate, in real time on a dashboard, the data a radio skill can capture on Alexa. No data is sold, shared with third parties, or used to serve real advertising.

Retention

All demonstration data is transient and is deleted when the demonstration ends.
